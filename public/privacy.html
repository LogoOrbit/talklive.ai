Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This policy explains what talklive.site ("TalkLive," "we," "us") collects when you use the service, why, and what choices you have. Using TalkLive means you agree to this policy — if you don't agree, please don't use the service.

1. What We Collect

TalkLive doesn't require an account to make a call, so we collect very little by default:

  • Connection info: your IP address and basic device/browser details, used briefly to match you with another user and estimate a rough location (country/city) for display.
  • Usage data: standard technical logs (timestamps, referrers, error data) used to keep the service running and to spot abuse.
  • Cookies & local storage: small bits of data stored in your browser to remember settings like your preferences, and to support analytics/ads.
  • Call audio: your microphone audio is streamed peer-to-peer (WebRTC) directly to the person you're matched with. It does not pass through or get stored on our servers, and we don't record calls.
  • Chat messages: relayed live during a call and not stored once the session ends.
  • Account info (optional): if you sign in (e.g. with Google) or subscribe to Premium, we store the identifiers needed to run your account and subscription — see Sections 6–7.

2. AI-Based Voice Gender Estimation

To power optional features like gender-based match filtering for Premium users, TalkLive may run your live voice through an AI model that estimates gender in real time.

  • Your audio is analyzed in the moment and immediately discarded — we do not save recordings.
  • The resulting estimate may be held briefly in your browser's local storage and in server memory for the length of your session only, and is never linked to your identity or kept past that session.
  • It's used solely to enable the matching feature you've opted into, not for profiling, advertising, or any automated decision that affects you beyond that immediate match.
  • The estimate is AI-generated and won't always be accurate.

3. How We Use Information

We use the data above to operate matchmaking, run optional features (like gender filtering), maintain and improve the service, prevent abuse and enforce our rules, respond to support requests, and — where you've agreed to it — show relevant ads or send you updates. Reported audio/behavior may be reviewed by automated systems or staff to enforce our Community Guidelines.

4. Sharing With Third Parties

We don't sell your personal data. We share the minimum necessary information with:

  • Google — if you choose to sign in with Google, to authenticate you.
  • Freemius — our payment processor, to handle Premium subscriptions, billing, and refunds.
  • Analytics/ad partners — aggregated, non-identifying usage data to help us understand traffic and (where applicable) show ads.
  • Authorities — only where required by law, to investigate fraud/abuse, or to protect the safety of users or the service.

5. Age Requirement

TalkLive is for users 18 and older. We don't knowingly allow anyone under 18 to use the service, and accounts found to belong to a minor will be terminated and any associated data deleted.

6. Premium Features & Billing Data

If you subscribe to Premium, we (via Freemius) store your subscription status, billing identifiers, and login/session metadata needed to provision and support the feature. Payment card details are handled directly by Freemius, not by us.

7. Data Retention

We keep account and billing data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal/tax obligations (billing records may be retained longer where required by law). You can request deletion of your data at any time — see Section 9.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data, but no online service can guarantee absolute security. If we become aware of a breach affecting you, we'll notify you as required by law.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export the data we hold about you, object to certain processing, or opt out of having your data shared for advertising. To exercise any of these, contact us at the address below — we'll verify your request and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

10. Do Not Track

Browser "Do Not Track" signals aren't yet standardized, so we don't currently respond to them. You can still manage cookies and tracking through your browser and device settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date; continuing to use TalkLive after a change means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at info@talklive.site.
