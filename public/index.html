Terms & Community Guidelines

How TalkLive Works

TalkLive connects you with a random stranger for a live, audio-only conversation. There's no sign-up — tap "Start," allow microphone access, and you'll be paired with someone automatically. Tap "Next" any time to end the current call and get matched with someone new, or "Hang Up" to leave entirely. You can optionally set a gender preference, a country, and interest tags from the settings (⚙️) menu to help find a better match.

Quick Tips

  • Use headphones to avoid echo/feedback for both you and your match.
  • You can mute your mic at any time with the microphone button.
  • Text chat is available alongside audio via the chat icon.
  • If a call feels unsafe or inappropriate, tap "Report" — this immediately ends the call and blocks that person from being matched with you again.

Community Guidelines — Be a Good Stranger

TalkLive only works if people treat each other with basic respect. Please:

  • Be kind. Treat the person on the other end the way you'd want to be treated.
  • Keep things audio-only and consensual — don't pressure others into topics, content, or behavior they haven't agreed to.
  • Respect a "no" or a hang-up. If someone ends the call or asks you to stop, let it go.
  • Assume you're talking to a real stranger, possibly a minor despite the age requirement below — act accordingly.
  • Use the Report button if something feels wrong. Reporting helps keep this usable for everyone.

Prohibited Conduct

The following will get you reported and banned, and may be referred to law enforcement where applicable:

  • Sexual content, sexual harassment, or solicitation of any kind.
  • Hate speech, slurs, or harassment based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality.
  • Threats, intimidation, or incitement of violence.
  • Any use of TalkLive by, or directed at, anyone under 18.
  • Recording, screenshotting, or distributing audio from a call without the other person's consent.
  • Spam, advertising, bots, scripts, or automated use of the service.
  • Impersonation, scams, phishing, or attempts to collect personal information from other users.
  • Any activity that is illegal in your jurisdiction.

Ban Policy — Read This

Reports are taken seriously. If you're reported for prohibited conduct, the reporting user is permanently blocked from being matched with you again, and repeated or severe reports can result in your access to TalkLive being suspended or permanently banned, with no entitlement to a refund of any kind (TalkLive is free, but this applies to any future paid features). We reserve the right to ban any user, at any time, for any reason connected to abuse of the service or harm to other users, without prior notice. Attempting to evade a ban (new device, new network, new account signal) is itself a violation and may result in a permanent IP-level block.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information TalkLive collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the Service, you consent to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

No registration required. You can use TalkLive's core audio matching without providing any personal information, account, email address, or phone number.

IP address & approximate location. We read your IP address to estimate your country/city (via an offline geo-lookup) so we can display it and use it for country-based matching. This happens in memory for the duration of your connection and is not written to a persistent database.

Device/browser identifier. On your first visit, a random identifier is generated and stored in your browser's local storage. It is used only to enforce report-based blocks and bans consistently across sessions on the same device, and to remember preferences like your nickname, friends list, and auto-call setting.

Match preferences. Any gender, country, and interest preferences you set are used only to find you a better match and are not linked to an identity.

Optional account data. If you choose to create an account, we store your username, a securely hashed version of your password (never in plain text), and an optional nickname, solely to let you log back in and manage your settings.

Audio. Call audio travels directly between you and your match over a peer-to-peer WebRTC connection. It does not pass through or get stored on our servers, and we do not record it.

Text chat. Chat messages sent during a call are relayed live, in memory, directly to your matched partner and are not stored after the session ends.

Call history. Your recent-calls list is kept only in your browser for the current session and clears when you close or reload the page — it is not sent to or stored on our servers.
