About TalkLive

TalkLive is a voice-first social platform that connects you with a random stranger somewhere in the world for a live, anonymous audio conversation. One tap, no sign-up, no video — just a real human voice on the other end of the line.

Our Mission

The internet made it easy to scroll past millions of people and hard to actually talk to one. TalkLive exists to bring back spontaneous, genuine conversation: the kind you'd have with a stranger on a train, except the train spans the whole planet. We believe voice — without profiles, photos, or pressure — is the most honest way for two strangers to meet.

What Makes TalkLive Different

  • Audio only. No camera anxiety, no appearance judgments. You connect through what you say, not how you look.
  • Anonymous by design. No real name, email, or phone number is required. You appear as a display name you choose.
  • Private calls. Audio travels peer-to-peer via WebRTC — conversations are never recorded or stored on our servers.
  • Real safety tools. Instant report and block, automatic bans for repeat offenders, and a strict 18+ policy.
  • Friends, not followers. When a conversation clicks, add each other as friends and call back later — no phone numbers exchanged.
  • Truly global. A 12-language interface with right-to-left support, and people online from dozens of countries at any moment.

How It Works

Tap the green button, allow microphone access, and our matching server pairs you with a waiting stranger — optionally filtered by country preferences. Your voice then flows directly between the two of you. Tap Next any time to meet someone new, or Hang Up to leave. That's the whole product, on purpose.

Free and Premium

TalkLive's core experience — unlimited random voice calls — is free. TalkLive Premium ($10/month) unlocks every match filter, unlimited friends, instant matching with no wait, and an ad-free experience for people who want the most out of the platform.
