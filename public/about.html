About us

Real Voices. Real People. Anywhere on Earth.

TalkLive exists because the best conversations happen when two strangers simply talk — no profiles to judge, no photos to swipe, no pressure to perform.

Our mission

We believe voice is the most human way to connect online. TalkLive strips social interaction back to its essentials: one tap, one stranger, one honest conversation. Whether you want to practice a language, hear a perspective from another continent, or just aren't ready to sleep yet — someone in the world is up for a chat.

What we stand for

Privacy first

No real names, no phone numbers, no video, no recordings. You control what you share, always.

Free for everyone

Matching, calling, friends, and messaging are free. No paywall between you and a good conversation.

Safety, seriously

Live moderation, one-tap reporting, instant blocking, and automatic bans. Our community guidelines are simple: be kind.

Truly worldwide

Available in 12 languages with users across every timezone, so there's always someone to talk to.

How it works

Tap the orb. TalkLive matches you with another person looking to talk right now — optionally filtered by gender, country, and shared interests. The call runs peer-to-peer over encrypted WebRTC audio. When it ends, tap again for someone new, or add the person as a friend to message them and call back later.
