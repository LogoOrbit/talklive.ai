Frequently asked questions

How does billing work?

Payments are processed securely by Paddle, our merchant of record. Your subscription renews monthly at $10 until you cancel. TalkLive never sees your card details.

How do I cancel?

Use the management link in the receipt email Paddle sends you at purchase, or email info@talklive.app. Premium stays active until the end of your paid month.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — your first payment is fully refundable within 14 days, no questions asked. See the full Refund Policy.

Does Premium work without an account?

Yes. Premium is tied to your unique TalkLive ID in this browser (shown in Settings). Upgrade from the same browser/device you use to talk.
