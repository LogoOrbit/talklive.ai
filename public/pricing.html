Frequently asked questions

How does billing work?

With the current 50% offer your subscription renews monthly at $5 (normally $10) until you cancel. Checkout is handled securely — TalkLive never sees your card details.

How do I cancel?

Use the management link in the receipt email you get at purchase, or email info@talklive.app. Premium stays active until the end of your paid month.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — your first payment is fully refundable within 14 days, no questions asked. See the full Refund Policy.

Does Premium work without an account?

Yes. Premium is tied to your unique TalkLive ID in this browser (shown in Settings). Upgrade from the same browser/device you use to talk.
