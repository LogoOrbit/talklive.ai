Frequently asked questions

Are international calls on TalkLive free?

Yes. All calls use your internet connection, so talking to someone in another country is completely free.

Can I choose which countries to talk to?

Yes. Optional country filters let you include or exclude specific countries so you connect with the regions you want.

What languages does TalkLive support?

The interface is available in twelve languages including English, Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, Hindi, Urdu, Chinese and more, with full right-to-left support.

Do I need a phone number to call internationally?

No. International calls happen inside the app with no number required, keeping your identity private.

Will it work on a slow connection?

Yes. TalkLive is audio-only and lightweight, so it performs well even on slower international networks.
